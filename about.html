About EmmanuelIyke-Foundation

We stand in the gap as God’s extension to restore hope

In a world where not everyone had access to basic needs such as education, healthcare, and community services, there was a group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a difference. They were determined to create a world where everyone had the opportunity to reach their full potential and lead fulfilling lives, regardless of their economic status or social background.

Thus, Emmanuel Iyke Foundation was born, a non-profit organization committed to ensuring the basic needs of less privilege children and young people are achieved with focus on the most vulnerable in the society.

Our mission was simple – to ensure the availability and accessibility of every child basic needs and to empower young people to acquire knowledge and skills in their area of interest, thereby fostering long-term growth and sustainability.

As we embarked on our journey, we faced many challenges. We saw children who lacked access to quality education, talented individuals who could not pursue higher education due to financial constraints, and communities that lacked access to healthcare services. It was clear that there was much work to be done.
But we didn’t give up. We knew that by working together, we could make a positive impact on the world. We rolled up our sleeves and got to work, offering a variety of programs to support our mission.

MISSION

to create a society where the fundamental rights of underprivileged and vulnerable children and young individuals are fully acknowledged and fulfilled. We strive to empower them by providing expanded opportunities for growth, enabling them to thrive and achieve their utmost potential.

VISION

to diligently work towards guaranteeing the availability and accessibility of essential needs for every child, while simultaneously empowering young individuals to acquire knowledge and develop skills in their respective areas of interest. We are wholeheartedly committed to making a positive impact and fostering an environment where children and youth can thrive and succeed.
